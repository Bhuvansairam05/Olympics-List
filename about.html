About the Olympic Medals


This project provides a real-time overview of medals won by various countries across different Olympic sports. This platform ensures transparent and up-to-date information about ongoing competitions, allowing users to track the performance of nations and athletes. Our mission is to celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship and global unity by offering a user-friendly interface to explore medal statistics, discover popular sports, and witness historical trends in Olympic events. Whether you're a sports enthusiast, a data lover, or just curious about the world's biggest sporting event, this project aims to offer engaging insights and live updates that matter.
